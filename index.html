Hi, I'm Sandesh Amgai

Learner, Engineer, Poet and Educator

Undergraduate studies far away from home has been a roller coaster ride for me, but my desire to learn has always had its upward trajectory. I plan to pursue graduate studies as soon as I complete my baccalaureate to continue with my hunt in making this world a better place to live.
